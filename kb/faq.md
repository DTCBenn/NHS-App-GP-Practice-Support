# NHS App Staff FAQ (local)

## General guardrails
- For staff support only
- Do not enter patient-identifiable information
- Non-clinical guidance only
- If in doubt, escalate via your local process

## Common issues
### Patient cannot log in
What to check:
- Are they using NHS login (not the GP website login)
- Are they entering the correct email/phone used for NHS login
- Can they complete NHS login recovery steps
Practice action:
- Confirm patient demographic details are accurate on PDS where applicable
Patient action:
- Use NHS login recovery steps (reset password, verify email/phone)

### GP record not connected
Typical causes:
- Not fully verified for access level
- Practice not linked or mismatch in details
- Recent registration changes
Practice action:
- Confirm registration status and details are correct
Patient action:
- Ensure correct practice selected and verification completed

### Proxy access
Typical causes:
- Proxy relationship not fully set up
- Patient or proxy details mismatch
- Access level limitations
Practice action:
- Follow local proxy setup and verification steps
Patient action:
- Confirm NHS login identity and correct account
